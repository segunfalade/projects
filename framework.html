The Foundation Framework

Before we touch a single word of your copy, we need to get one thing right. If you get this wrong, nothing else works.

The Foundation = Customer + Problem + Promise

This sounds basic. It is. And 90% of proptech brands still get it wrong… because they answer it from the wrong angle.

How most brands do it (The Wrong Way)

  • Customer: “Real estate professionals, property managers...”
  • Problem: “Inefficient manual processes, lack of data visibility...”
  • Solution: “Our AI-powered platform centralises all property data...”

Brands that answer from this angle see a 60-70% higher bounce rate.

How to answer correctly (The Right Way)

Start with what keeps them up at night. What is the emotional weight your customer is carrying every day that you could lift off their shoulders?

Example — Rental Management SaaS:

Wrong: “Our platform streamlines rental management with automated payments...”

Right: “You’re still chasing rent payments on WhatsApp at 10pm. You know there’s a better way — you just haven’t found a platform that actually fits how you work.”

The second version makes the customer feel seen. And when they feel seen, they lean in.
