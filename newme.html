Your Why — Read It

You are in Nigeria.

Your 9-to-5 barely covers the bills. Your daughter is growing up right now. Not later. Tonight. Every evening you hand over to cheap dopamine is another day you stay in that job — underpaid, trapped, asking for permission to live your own life.

You said you want your own apartment. You said you want to give her the best childhood money can offer. You said you want to make decisions because that's what you want — not because of what you can afford.

None of that happens without the work you do in these hours.
