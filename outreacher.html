Trust Funnel Diagnostic Tool 🩻✨

15-Minute Sweep to identify where trust hemorrhages.

1) Entry Point Audit (3 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Check where first contact happens: LinkedIn/X content, founder bio, homepage, lead magnet, ads, podcasts.

Ask: What promise is being made here? (e.g., "close property deals faster", "reduce fraud risk")

Key Question: Does the first promise survive the click?
If the post promises certainty but the landing page becomes vague corporate soup ("modern digital infrastructure"), curiosity dies.
2) Landing Page Trust Check (4 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Check Clarity (5-second rule): What this is, who it's for, why it matters now.

Check Credibility: Proof, numbers, case studies, logos, trust badges, compliance.
*Absence of proof in proptech/fintech is a red flare 🚨

Objection Handling: What fear would a buyer naturally have? (scam risk, data security, hidden fees). Is it addressed?

Consultant Note: If unaddressed, label as "core buyer objection left unanswered."
3) CTA Pathway Audit (3 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Action: Click every CTA and map where each button goes.
  • Book Demo -> Calendly (Good)
  • Learn More -> Generic homepage (Weak)
  • Get Started -> Signup wall (Friction)
Key Question: Does the CTA deepen certainty or interrupt momentum?
Sending a warm prospect to a generic homepage is like making someone ready to buy go back to the parking lot.
4) Form + Lead Capture Friction (2 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Check: Number of fields, unclear labels, weak reassurance text.

Bad: "Submit your details"
Better: "Get your fraud-risk audit in 24 hours" (preserves value narrative).

Silent Leak Check: Is there privacy reassurance? For trust-first industries, this matters heavily.
5) Follow-Up Sequence Test (4 to 5 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Action: Opt in with a spare email and watch what happens.

Audit: Instant confirmation? Reminder email? Nurture/trust-building sequence? Is there sales pressure too early?

Key Question: Does the follow-up continue the psychological journey?
If the LP builds safety but the email screams "BOOK NOW!", that's tonal whiplash.
6) Sales Handoff / Demo Flow (2 mins)
➕ View Analysis Checklist
Check what happens before the call: Confirmation page, founder intro video, prep email, case study link, expectations set.

If none exists, note: "High-intent leads enter sales call without belief reinforcement."
